About Me

I am a passionate data analyst dedicated to empowering organizations through effective data utilization. Armed with a bachelor's degree in marketing and logistics, a minor in data analytics, and a recent data scientist professional certification, I bring four years of expertise to the field. My journey began at Unilever, a global CPG company, where I excelled in extracting, analyzing, and presenting intricate data to drive informed decision-making. Continuing this trajectory at ABCS Insights, a startup in market research, I played a pivotal role in integrating data analytics into our services. At ABCS Insights, I had the privilege of collaborating with some of the biggest media clients globally, including Conde Nast, Warner Media Discovery, Paramount Global. I performed analyses for leading companies spanning various industries such as Starbucks, Wendy's, Nestle, Unilever, ABI, Anheuser-Busch, P&G, Quaker, MGM Resorts, Carmax, Delta, State Farm, Pepsico, Subaru, and more.
